# Efficient Multimodal Question Answering (EMM-QA)

Efficient multimodal question answering in the era of large language models.

> [Call for Papers](/competition/2026/call-for-papers/)

## Positioning

EMM-QA is centered on efficient multimodal question answering, with an emphasis on systems that perform well under practical resource constraints while handling knowledge-intensive tasks across multiple input modalities.

The scope is **medium-broad**. We welcome closely related work on multimodal retrieval, reasoning, evaluation, benchmarking, and efficient inference when those contributions are clearly connected to question answering or other knowledge-intensive multimodal tasks.

## Topics of Interest

Relevant themes include:

- Efficient multimodal question answering
- Large language models for QA
- Open-domain QA under resource constraints
- Multimodal QA over text + image, text + table, text + sound
- Efficient retrieval and answer lookup
- Synthetic data generation for efficient QA
- Balancing accuracy vs. model or system size
- Token-efficiency and compute-efficiency in QA systems
- Human verification and evaluation of QA outputs

## Audience

The target audience includes researchers working on question answering, multimodal machine learning and NLP, retrieval, efficient modeling, and LLMs across both academia and industry.
