## What makes QANTA 2026 multimodal?

Quiz bowl questions are already pyramid-style: they begin with harder clues and become easier as the question continues. In QANTA 2026, some of those clues are images rather than text alone. A question might include a painting detail, scientific diagram, map, photograph, table, or other visual evidence alongside the text clues.

That format lets us study:

- Visual reasoning
- Cross-modal fusion between text and images
- Calibration under uncertainty
- Human-AI collaboration during live question answering

## Why is there no monetary prize for the most helpful computer teammate?

We are not yet confident that we can measure "most helpful" fairly enough to attach a monetary prize to it.

Helpfulness in a live human-computer setting depends on more than raw answer accuracy. It can involve timing, calibration, how often a system should stay quiet, whether it helps a team avoid bad answers, and how different human teams choose to use it. We plan to recognize notable systems for helping human teams, but we do not want to turn that into a cash prize until the measurement is stable and transparent.
