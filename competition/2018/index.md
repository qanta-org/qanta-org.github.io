# 2018 NeurIPS Human–Computer Question Answering Competition

Co-located with NeurIPS 2018, the 2018 QANTA competition brought together human and computer teams in a full quiz bowl tournament. The team **"Rage Against the Machine"** claimed first place.

## Context

This competition also featured the **"Trick Me 2"** adversarial question writing event, where writers crafted questions specifically designed to stump the QANTA system. See the [Adversarial QA project page](/research/projects/adversarial/) for details and data.
