## 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Play as a Human

Bring your knowledge, strategy, and intuition — then team up with an AI agent to outthink the competition. See how human–AI collaboration stacks up against solo play.

## 📋 Rules

Games follow [ACF Rules](https://acf-quizbowl.com/gameplay-rules/) with modifications described in the [Rules Exception Document](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1i_jGlmL7ygsSUU6X0r56s91GSADhumq832FhriRUIJ0/edit?usp=sharing).

---

## How It Works

1. Human players answer quiz bowl tossup questions
2. An AI teammate provides guesses and explanations alongside yours
3. You decide when to buzz and how to factor in AI input
4. Teams are ranked by overall score across the competition

---

## Who Should Play?

QANTA welcomes quiz bowl players of all experience levels — from seasoned tournament players to casual trivia enthusiasts. No prior quiz bowl experience is required.
